Overview

Trailing Greenery for Overhead Impact

Our hanging vines and garlands add soft, cascading greenery to ceilings, pergolas, columns, and feature walls where rooted plants cannot grow. Each strand is built on a flexible wired stem and dressed with UV-stabilized PE/PU leaves for natural drape and movement.

From a single hanging strand to a full ceiling canopy, we manufacture vines by the metre in your chosen foliage, length, and fullness — then bundle the complete order for one consolidated shipment.

  • ✓Natural drape & movement — wired stems shape and trail like living vines.
  • ✓Made by the metre — continuous lengths cut and bundled to your spec.
  • ✓Foliage & flowering mixes — ivy, pothos, wisteria, and floral garlands.
  • ✓Indoor & outdoor grades — UV and NFPA 701 fire treatment on request.

Hanging Vines FAQ

How are the vines made and supplied?

Vines are manufactured by the metre on flexible wired stems with pre-finished end loops, then cut and bundled to your specified lengths for easy suspension.

Can you build a full ceiling canopy?

Yes. We manufacture grid-mounted canopy systems where vine strands are pre-attached to a suspension grid for fast install over large overhead areas.

Are the vines suitable for outdoor pergolas?

Our UV-stabilized outdoor grade is aging tested for pergolas, arbors, and covered exteriors. Standard grade is intended for interior use.

Do you offer flowering garlands?

We produce wisteria, rose, and blossom garlands alongside foliage vines, with custom bloom colors for events and reception features.

What is the minimum order and lead time?

MOQ is trade-friendly per style. Production runs 20-35 days depending on volume and canopy builds, then consolidated shipping to your port.
