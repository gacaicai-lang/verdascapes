Overview

Real Palm Presence, Zero Maintenance

Our artificial palm trees recreate the scale and silhouette of mature tropical palms without irrigation, pruning, or climate limits. Each tree is built on a load-rated steel or GRP trunk core and dressed with hand-applied bark texture and UV-stabilized PE fronds.

From a 1.5m lobby accent to an 8m feature palm for an atrium or poolside, every unit is made to your height, frond count, and trunk finish — and packed for container export to spec.

  • ✓Heights to 8m — date palms up to 8m, coconut & fan palms to spec.
  • ✓Outdoor UV grade — fronds and trunk hold color through sun exposure.
  • ✓Steel / GRP core — engineered to stand in wind-exposed and poolside settings.
  • ✓Fire-retardant option — NFPA 701 grade for enclosed commercial spaces.

Palm Tree FAQ

What is the tallest palm you can make?+

Date palms up to 8m as standard; taller custom builds are available as sectional units for on-site assembly.

Are they suitable for outdoor and poolside use?+

Yes. Outdoor-grade UV fronds and a galvanized steel or GRP core are designed for sun, wind, and pool environments.

What is the minimum order quantity?+

Flexible for projects — from single feature palms to full-container trade orders. Tell us your project scope for pricing.

Can you match a specific palm species or height?+

Yes — send a reference photo and target height and we'll quote a made-to-spec build.
