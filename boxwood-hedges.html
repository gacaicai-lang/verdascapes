Overview

Instant Privacy, Zero Maintenance

Our artificial boxwood delivers a dense, manicured evergreen look with none of the trimming, watering, or seasonal dieback of live hedging. Each panel is built on a flexible mesh backing layered with UV-stabilized PE foliage, so it stays sharp and green through full outdoor exposure.

Use it as fast privacy screening on fences and balconies, as a clean architectural border, or shaped into topiary forms for entrances and event spaces. We manufacture every panel, roll, and form in-house and ship the complete order as one project.

  • ✓Dense, manicured look — tight leaf coverage with realistic two-tone greens.
  • ✓Flexible mesh backing — zip-ties or staples to any fence, frame, or railing.
  • ✓Outdoor UV grade — color-fast through years of sun and weather.
  • ✓Topiary & custom forms — balls, cones, spirals, and animal shapes to spec.

Boxwood Hedge FAQ

How do the panels attach to a fence or railing?

The flexible mesh backing fixes with zip-ties, staples, or screws to any fence, frame, or railing. Panels interlock at the edges for continuous, seamless coverage.

Are the hedges suitable for full outdoor use?

Yes. Our standard boxwood is UV-stabilized and aging tested for full outdoor exposure on fence lines, balconies, and boundaries.

Can you produce custom topiary shapes?

We manufacture balls, cones, spirals, and sculpted animal or figure topiary on rigid frames. Send a reference image and dimensions for a quote.

Do you offer fire-rated screening for indoor use?

NFPA 701 fire-retardant grade is available on request, with test documentation for indoor commercial partitions and screening.

What is the minimum order and lead time?

MOQ for panels and rolls is trade-friendly. Production runs 25-40 days depending on volume and custom forms, then consolidated shipping to your port.
