Overview

The Building Blocks of Every Scape

Our faux greenery is the loose foliage that designers and contractors layer into every project — the stems, bushes, and fillers that dress planters, build arrangements, and complete green walls. Each piece is molded in UV-stabilized PE/PU on a wired stem for natural shaping and reuse across builds.

With 300+ foliage SKUs in stock, we supply by the carton at trade volumes — single varieties or pre-blended mixes — packed and consolidated for one efficient shipment to your studio or site.

  • ✓300+ foliage SKUs — leaves, stems, bushes, ferns, grasses, and flowers.
  • ✓Wired stems — bend, trim, and arrange to fit any planter or wall.
  • ✓Bulk carton packing — trade volumes priced and packed for projects.
  • ✓Indoor & outdoor grades — UV and NFPA 701 fire treatment on request.

Faux Greenery FAQ

How is the foliage packed and shipped?

Foliage is carton-packed by SKU or as pre-blended designer mixes, with compression-friendly stems for efficient freight and consolidated container shipping.

Can you supply pre-blended mixes?

Yes. We curate and pack ready-to-install foliage assortments to your design brief so planters and walls go together faster on site.

Is the foliage suitable for outdoor use?

Our UV-stabilized outdoor grade is aging tested for exterior layering. Standard grade is intended for interior arrangements and walls.

Do you offer custom branded packaging?

For OEM clients we produce branded polybag and retail packaging alongside bulk cartons. Send your artwork and pack format for a quote.

What is the minimum order and lead time?

MOQ is trade-friendly per SKU. Production runs 20-35 days depending on volume and custom mixes, then consolidated shipping to your port.
