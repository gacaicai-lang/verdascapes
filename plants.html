Overview

Lifelike Plants for Every Commercial Space

Our artificial plant collection brings botanical realism to lobbies, offices, restaurants, and patios without watering, light, or seasonal replacement. Each plant is molded from real leaf casts in UV-stabilized PE/PU and built on a steel-wired stem for natural shaping and long-term durability.

From tabletop succulents to two-metre floor specimens, we manufacture and pot every plant in-house — in your chosen species, height, and container — and ship the complete collection as one consolidated order.

  • ✓200+ realistic species — tropical, foliage, succulent, and flowering ranges.
  • ✓Steel-wired stems — bendable and reposeable for a natural silhouette.
  • ✓Custom potting — your planters, finishes, and weighted bases.
  • ✓Indoor & outdoor grades — UV and NFPA 701 fire treatment on request.

Artificial Plants FAQ

Do the plants arrive potted and ready to place?

We supply loose stems, nursery liners, or fully finished weighted pots in your chosen container — specify the option that fits your install and budget.

How many species can you produce?

Our standard range spans 200+ realistic species across tropical, foliage, succulent, and flowering categories, with custom species developed on request.

Can plants be used outdoors?

Yes. Our UV-stabilized outdoor grade is aging tested for patios, atriums, and covered exteriors. Standard grade is intended for interiors.

Do you offer fire-rated plants?

NFPA 701 fire-retardant grade is available on request with test documentation for code-driven commercial interiors.

What is the minimum order and lead time?

MOQ is trade-friendly per species. Production runs 25-40 days depending on volume and potting, then consolidated shipping to your port.
