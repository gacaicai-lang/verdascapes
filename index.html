Frequently Asked Questions

What's your minimum order for a turnkey project?

There's no fixed MOQ for complete projects — we've shipped everything from a single $3,000 courtyard to $200,000 commercial scapes. For individual product lines, MOQ starts around $5,000 mixed.

How long from design to delivery?

Design proposal in 3-5 days. Once approved, production + consolidation runs 30-45 days, then sea freight to your port. Express air options available for samples and small kits.

Do you ship worldwide and handle customs?

Yes — FOB, CIF, or DDP to 60+ countries. With DDP we handle freight, duties, and delivery to your door. You only unpack and install.

Can you match a specific design or render?

Send your moodboard, CAD, or site photos. Our design team returns a buildable proposal with a full bill of materials, samples, and compliance docs.

Are samples available before I commit?

Yes. Sample swatches and single units ship within days at unit price + courier. Sample cost is credited against your first bulk order.

Do you support installation?

Every shipment includes layout maps, fixing hardware, and video install guides. For large commercial projects we can arrange on-site supervision.
