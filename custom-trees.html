Overview

Statement Trees, Engineered to Scale

Our custom trees anchor lobbies, atriums, and outdoor plazas with the presence of mature specimens — without the weight, mess, or upkeep of live trees. Each tree is built around a welded steel trunk core, fitted with hand-carved bark and UV-stabilized PE/PU foliage for true-to-species realism.

From cherry blossom and wisteria to palms, banyans, and olive trees, we engineer every tree to your height, canopy spread, and base type — then knock it down for flat-pack shipping and rebuild it on site with our install guides.

  • ✓Welded steel trunk core — load-rated and wind-stable for large canopies.
  • ✓Hand-carved bark finish — natural texture molded to each species.
  • ✓Knock-down assembly — flat-pack freight and on-site rebuild.
  • ✓Indoor & outdoor grades — UV and NFPA 701 fire treatment on request.

Custom Trees FAQ

How tall can you build the trees?

Standard builds range from 2 m to 8 m. Larger custom heights are engineered to order with a welded steel core sized for the canopy load.

How are large trees shipped and installed?

Trees are engineered for knock-down assembly — trunk sections and branches flat-pack into the container and rebuild on site using our layout maps and fixing kits.

Can the trees be installed outdoors?

Yes. Our UV-stabilized outdoor grade is aging tested for plazas, courtyards, and covered exteriors with in-ground or weighted base options.

Do you build bespoke species?

Beyond our 200+ standard species we develop bespoke trees from reference images, with custom bark, canopy, and bloom to your design.

What is the lead time for custom trees?

Custom trees typically run 30-45 days depending on size and complexity, then consolidated shipping to your port.
