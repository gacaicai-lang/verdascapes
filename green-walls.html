Overview

Living-Look Walls, Engineered to Last

Our artificial green walls deliver the depth and richness of a real vertical garden without irrigation, lighting, or upkeep. Each system is built on a rigid injection-molded grid and layered with UV-stabilized PE/PU foliage, so color and texture hold under years of facade sun and high-traffic interiors.

From a single reception accent to a full multi-storey facade, we manufacture every panel in-house to your exact dimensions, density, and foliage mix — then consolidate, fire-treat, and ship the complete wall as one project order.

  • ✓Realistic multi-layer density — mixed leaf shapes and tones for a true botanical look.
  • ✓Click-lock modular build — fast install with seamless, joint-free coverage.
  • ✓Indoor & outdoor grades — facade-rated UV and NFPA 701 fire treatment on request.
  • ✓Full OEM/ODM — custom foliage palettes and branded logo integration.

Green Wall FAQ

What's the standard panel size and how do they join?

Standard modules are 50×50 cm with a click-lock grid and concealed fixing tabs, so panels join seamlessly with no visible seams. For full-wall builds we pre-fit panels to your drawings.

Can the walls be installed outdoors?

Yes. Our outdoor UV grade uses reinforced UV stabilizers and a facade backing system, third-party aging tested for exterior and rooftop exposure.

Do you offer fire-rated panels?

NFPA 701 fire-retardant grade is available on request, with test documentation supplied for code-driven commercial interiors.

Can you build a custom logo or brand wall?

We manufacture full OEM logo walls with inset lettering, crests, and brand color palettes. Send your logo vector and dimensions for a proposal.

What is the minimum order and lead time?

MOQ for panels starts low for trade clients. Production typically runs 25-40 days depending on volume and customization, then consolidated shipping to your port.
