Our Company

From Single Plants to Whole Landscapes

18 years of craft, one factory, and a simple promise: design it, build it, ship it — so you don't have to chase five suppliers.

Our Story

VerdaScapes began in 2007 as a single-product exporter — high-quality artificial plants shipped in bulk to wholesalers worldwide. For years we did one thing well: make realistic, durable faux greenery at factory-direct prices.

But our best clients kept asking for more. Landscape contractors wanted hedges, turf, and pots in one shipment. Designers wanted us to build the whole scene, not just supply the parts. So we transformed — from a plant supplier into a turnkey landscape solutions partner.

Today VerdaScapes designs, manufactures, and ships complete landscape packages to 60+ countries. One factory, one container, one point of contact — for the contractors, designers, and property owners who want the whole landscape done right, the first time.
